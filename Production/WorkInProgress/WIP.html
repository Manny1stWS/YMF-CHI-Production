WORK IN PROGRESS

Prepared by Manuel Herrera

Updated: 2/17/24

Make an excel with below columns so you can keep track of your orders. It is very important that you keep the WIP updated in a daily basis.

  1. Step 1 - PO Setup - Enter the following:
    1. YMF PO#
    2. Factory
    3. Production Coordinator
    4. Customer
    5. General Description
    6. Category
    7. Sourcing Coordinator
    8. PO Created On
    9. PO Sent to the Factory On
    10. Requested ETD
    11. ETA
    12. Destination Port
    13. Total SQM Ordered
  2. Step 2 - Proforma Invoice - Enter the following:
    1. PI Received On
    2. PI Signed On
    3. PI Signed By
    4. Deposit Date Sent
    5. Requested Booking Date
    6. Agreed ETD
    7. ETA (Update if ETD different than requested)
    8. Port Sailing From
    9. CBM
    10. Cost of Goods
  3. Step 3 - Production - Enter the following:
    1. Production CAD Requested On
    2. CAD Sent to Factory On
    3. CAD Approved On
    4. Packaging Form Sent On
    5. Packaging Sent To Factory On
    6. Lab Dips
    7. Pre Production Required
    8. Pre Production Submitted On
  4. Step 4 - Customer Order - Enter the following:
    1. PO Finalized On
    2. Revised ETD
    3. Actual Booking Date
    4. ETA (Update if ETD is revised)
    5. Customer PO
    6. Customer Ship Date
    7. Customer Cancel Date
    8. In Exenta
    9. Exenta Updated On
    10. Tickets Ordered On
  5. Step 5 - Inspection - Enter the following:
    1. Inspection Appointment Date
    2. Inspector
    3. Inspection Report Received On
    4. Inspection Report Approved On
  6. Step 6 - Shipping - Enter the following:
    1. Actual Loading Date
    2. Actual Sailing Date
    3. ETA (Update based on the actual sailing date in Packing List)
    4. Container No
    5. Total SQM Shipped
    6. Remaining SQM To Ship
  7. Step 7 - Final PMT Process - Enter the following:
    1. PI Payment Sent On
    2. Completed
    3. YMF Notes
