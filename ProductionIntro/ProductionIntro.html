Company Overview

Operations Overview

  1. Order Processing:

    1. Department Store sales orders are received via various channels, including email, SPS Website (EDI), and electronically directly into Exenta.
    2. There are six types of Department Store sales orders: Direct Import, POE, MDDP, Replenishment, One Time Deals and FOB YMF NJ-CA Warehouse Stock.

  2. Order Types definition:

    1. Direct Import orders are picked up from country of origin’s port.
    2. POE orders are delivered to the customer’s preferred US port.
    3. MDDP orders are booked with Customer’s preferred carrier but unlike Direct Import orders YMF is responsible for customs clearance.
    4. Replenishment orders are sold from inventory from our warehouses. Purchase orders are placed with the factory depending on our Inventory level to be able to ship the customer sales orders. Jian Wang (Inventory Analyst) provides the quantities needed. Replenishment orders are typically shipped to our warehouses in NJ or CA.
    5. One-time deals or fashion buys occur annually or seasonally.
    6. FOB YMF NJ-CA Warehouse Stock orders are managed by Jonathan Shemesh.

  3. Warehouse facilities:

    1. Main warehouse: YMF Carpets, Inc. 201B Middlesex Center Blvd, Monroe Township, NJ 08831
    2. 3rd Party warehouse: Vie Logistics, 2201 E Carson St, Carson, CA 90810

  4. Purchasing and Supply Chain:

    1. Goods are sourced from our own factory (Elite Qingdao) in China or outsourced from various other factories in China or other countries like India, Turkey, Egypt, etc.
